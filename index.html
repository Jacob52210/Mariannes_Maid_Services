I dust, mop, vacuum, wash, dry, and put away dishes, I wash and fold laundry. My rates are $30 an hour and 50 cents a mile outside of a 10 mile radius.

About Me

My name is Marianne C Bryan. I am the owner and operator of Marianne's Maid Service. I care about the health and well being of other's so I have decided to use my skills to clean homes for other people that can not or does not have the extra time. I graduated from Bryant and Stratton college in 2007 as a medical assistant. I was working in the medical field up until the loss of my late husband in 2017. I am trained in sterilization and organizing as well as being a mother to two children and a grandmother to 5 grandchildren. I typically try to use homemade remedies for my cleaning but due to the pandemic I have been having to use Lysol and bleach. Outside of cleaning homes I like to spend quality time with my children and grandchildren as well as fishing and camping. I also enjoy cooking (I'm told I'm pretty good at cooking)
